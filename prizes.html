Judging

How we pick winners

We're not grading your code. We're asking: "Would I actually use this? Would I tell someone about it? Does it make me feel something?"

What matters:

  • Delight: Does it spark joy or just exist?
  • Usability: Can someone figure it out without a tutorial?
  • Clarity: Does it solve one thing well or five things poorly?
  • Shareability: Would you post it in a channel unprompted?

A beautiful one-liner beats an ugly enterprise solution. Every time.
