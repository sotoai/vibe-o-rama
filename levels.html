Progression

Levels

No tests. No certifications. No gatekeeping. You level up by building and sharing. That's it.

Levels are about momentum, not mastery. Nobody's checking your homework. You do the thing, you get the badge.

The path

Five levels. Your pace.

Everyone starts at 1. Move as fast or slow as you want. No deadlines. No pressure.

L1Curious

You showed up. You're poking around. You're asking "wait, can it do that?"

What gets you here

  • 1Get your tools set up (Claude Code, Cursor)
  • 2Make something—anything—work
  • 3Post it in the channel even if it's embarrassing
L2Tinkerer

You've broken things. You've fixed things. You're starting to see patterns.

What gets you here

  • 1Build something that kinda works
  • 2Show it to someone and actually listen to their feedback
  • 3Try a different approach when the first one fails
L3Maker

You shipped something. Someone used it. They didn't hate it.

What gets you here

  • 1Publish an artifact people can actually use
  • 2Get 3+ people to try it and tell you what sucks
  • 3Write down what you learned (a few sentences is fine)
L4Systems Thinker

You're not building tools anymore. You're building workflows. Big difference.

What gets you here

  • 1Automate something you do every week
  • 2Connect your artifact to other tools
  • 3Help someone else level up their build
L5Vibe Lord

People are remixing your stuff. You're teaching without trying. You get it.

What gets you here

  • 1Create something others build on top of
  • 2Run a session or write a guide
  • 3Ship multiple artifacts across categories

Questions you might have

Do I need to reach Level 5?

Nope. Level 1 is a win. Level 2 is a bigger win. Level 5 is for people who can't stop.

Who decides when I level up?

Mostly you. If you did the things, you earned it. Facilitators can help if you're unsure.

Can I speedrun this?

If you're already building and sharing, you might move fast. But it's not a race.

What if I get stuck?

Post in the channel. Someone will help. That's literally the point.

Start at Level 1. See what happens.

The only prerequisite is showing up.
